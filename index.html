The Temple of Saint Sava

The Church of Saint Sava in Belgrade is the largest Serbian Orthodox church and is the second largest Orthodox church in the Balkans, after the Church of the People's Salvation in Bucharest, and the third largest in the world.

Kalemegdan Fortress

Kalemegdan covers 66 hectares and is at the same time the most important cultural and historical complex in the capital city. It is dominated by the Belgrade Fortress located at the confluence of the Sava and Danube rivers, which gives this building a special strategic importance and position.

National Assembly

The first law on the National Assembly of Serbia was passed on October 28, 1858.

New Year's eve

Nikola Tesla Museum

The museum has about 1,200 objects distributed in nine collections: Electrical engineering; mechanical engineering; Chemical technologies; Textiles and leather; Nikola Tesla's personal items; Orders, medals and other awards; Art objects from the bequest of Nikola Tesla and Art objects of the museum.

National Museum

Located on Republic Square, the National Museum is the oldest and most important museum in Serbia, founded in 1844. Its collection numbers over 400,000 exhibits, including works from prehistory, the Middle Ages, the Renaissance and the Baroque, as well as modern art.

Military Museum

Founded in 1878, this is one of the oldest museums in Serbia. Military items are stored in it, including different types of weapons, uniforms, flags, decorations, and in its yard, samples of artillery weapons are displayed. The museum is located almost in the very center of the Kalemegdan fortress, in the building of the former Military Geographical Institute, and is therefore an indispensable part of a tour to get to know Belgrade.

Historical museum

The Historical Museum of Serbia has grown over several decades of existence into a reputable and publicly recognizable institution of national importance with an important cultural, historical and educational role.

Aviation Museum

The Belgrade Aviation Museum, one of the leading museums of its type in the world, was founded in 1957.
